noexcept

Returns a mask that protects different part of the special register.

The special register is divided up to 4 parts: flag bits, status bits, extension bits, and control bits (see https://www.ques10.com/p/54453/explain-functions-of-different-registers-availab-1/).

Returns
Mask to protect different parts of the special register when setting a new value to it
